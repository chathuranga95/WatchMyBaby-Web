About Watch My Baby

Your Baby Monitor

Watch My Baby is a baby monitoring system with some extra features. It is consisting of this Web application and Watch My Baby Android app. You can Monitor your baby remotely with this Web App coupled with the Watch My Baby app. You can download the Android app from the play store with this link.

Features:

  • Watch the baby live - View Baby function in Android App
  • Automatically detecting baby cry and send notifications to the Android app
  • Receive notifications from your Android appp even when the app is closed and display is turned off
  • Upload and Schedule lullabys from the Android app and play them to your baby with the Web app
  • Automatically send SMS alerts to your neighbours when you are busy. (Settings available)

Quick How To Use:

  • Download the Watch My baby Android app Here

  • Create a new User account
  • Login to both Android and Web apps using your new user name and password
  • When you login in to the Web app, you have to give permission to access your Web cam and the microphone.
  • You can click the 'Camera preview' button on the web app and adjust the camera to view your Baby
  • That's it. Just click the 'View Baby' function in the Android app and you can monitor your baby.
  • To learn more about Watch My Baby and its functionalities, please refer to the official documentation of the Watch My Baby system
